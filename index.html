The Celliverse

Cells — tiny universes. Pick your mission: Animal cell (Arctic Fox) or Plant cell (Venus Flytrap).

Start: Arctic Fox (Animal Cell) Start: Venus Flytrap (Plant Cell)

Each mission contains 10 organelles with mini-games. Finish them to unlock the final adaptation transformation.

What to expect

  • Interactive organelle mini-games with cute animations
  • Adaptation meters that change the cell visually
  • Two adaptation storylines: Arctic Fox & Venus Flytrap
  • Mission log & evolution finale
Made for classroom projects. Host on GitHub Pages for best results.
